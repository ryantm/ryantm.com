No files, a prediction
2 May 2007

There will be no files in the future. Files are an artifact of computer construction. Files face many user interface problems. Here are the problems with files:

  1. Location—You have to know where they are, and you can't always get to them.
  2. Volatility—Files get lost, stolen, and corrupted.
  3. Context—Where a file is effects what it does.
